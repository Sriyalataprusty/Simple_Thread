Customer Analysis

During Q1 2025, Simple Threads saw 22 unique customers out of a total of 40 customers who made purchases, resulting in a 45% repeat customer rate, indicating high customer satisfaction.

Areal Distribution:

Customers are distributed across various cosmopolitan towns in India, including Ahmedabad, Surat, Jaipur, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Delhi, and Mumbai. While most cities are in North India, Bengaluru and Hyderabad represent the South, indicating a broad reach.

Gender Distribution:

Male 41.8% Female 58.2%

A significant majority of our customers are female (58.2%), suggesting our products strongly appeal to this demographic. Future strategies could involve focusing more on female customers or investing in R&D for more appealing male products.

Age Distribution:

Most customers in their 10s and 20s are females, indicating a strong appeal to younger women. In the 40-60 age group, the male-to-female ratio is more balanced, suggesting a similar focus on both genders for these age brackets. The overall age range is 18-64, with most customers in their 20s, 30s, and 40s, pointing to a mature and urban customer base.

Sales Analysis

Top Performing Products:

Women's apparel dominated sales in Q1 2025. The best-selling product was the **women's skirt**, with the **green skirt** being particularly popular. This was followed by **women's t-shirts**, and then men's shirts and t-shirts.

Revenue Distribution:

New Customer 16.2% Returning Customer 83.8%

The total revenue generated in Q1 2025 was ₹22,770. A significant 83.8% of this revenue came from returning customers, while new customers contributed 16.2%. This high contribution from returning customers highlights strong product satisfaction and loyalty.

Campaign Analysis

Campaign Impact:

Discount campaigns had a massive impact, generating approximately ₹16,570 in revenue, which is significantly higher than organic sales. The campaign contributed a substantial 72.8% of the total revenue during this period, making it highly effective.

Coupon Conversion:

Purchased 30.6% Ignored 69.4%

Out of customers who received coupons, 11 made a purchase, leading to a 30.6% conversion rate. This rate is notably higher than the industry average, showcasing the significant impact of the campaign.

The code "QAZWSX" was the most used, applied 3 times. "ZXCVBN", "TGBYHN", and "ASDFGH" were the most profitable discount codes, each generating ₹1,530 in revenue.

The correlation between sales amount and discount is a weak 0.061. This indicates that an increase in discount does not strongly lead to a significant increase in sales. This suggests that while discount campaigns drive overall revenue, the *depth* of the discount itself isn't the primary driver for higher purchase amounts.

Recommendations & Future Outlook

Based on the Q1 2025 analysis, here are the strategic recommendations for Simple Threads:

  • Expand Women's Apparel Offerings: Focus on increasing the variety and availability of women's skirts and shirts, as these categories are significant revenue drivers.
  • Optimize Discount Strategy: Shift from blanket discounts to targeted, moderated discounts applied to specific purchase amounts or above. This will leverage the success of campaigns while improving discount effectiveness.
  • Increase Average Sale Per Customer: Implement strategies like curated product bundles and loyalty incentives to encourage higher spending per order, thereby improving profit margins.
  • Improve Customer Data Integration: Align customer IDs with deeper demographic and behavioral analysis. Implement real-time data integration to address missing customer data from purchase information, enabling more precise targeting.
  • Target Key Regions: Increase marketing efforts and inventory in high-performing cities like Jaipur, Bengaluru, Kolkata, and Hyderabad to capitalize on existing customer bases.

Long-Term Vision:

Simple Threads aims for sustained growth by continuously adapting to market trends, innovating in sustainable fashion, and strengthening customer relationships. Our commitment to ethical practices and data-driven decisions will guide our path to becoming a leader in the apparel industry.
